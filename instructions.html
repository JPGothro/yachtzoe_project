Game Instructions

Welcome to YachtZoe!, the original dice game. This game is in no way affiliated with its popular knockoff "Yahtzee". To win, you must roll five dice in various combinations to get the highest score possible.


Each player may roll the dice up to 3 times per turn to get the highest scoring combination. The player must select zero or one of the 13 combination categories to complete their turn.


Additionally, players may choose to hold certain dice during their turn and roll the remaining until their turn is complete.


The game ends when all players have completed their score table.

Combination Name Description
Ones Total of 1's. Up to 5 points possible.
Twos Total of 2's. Up to 10 points possible.
Threes Total of 3's. Up to 15 points possible.
Fours Total of 4's. Up to 20 points possible.
Fives Total of 5's. Up to 25 points possible.
Sixes Total of 6's. Up to 30 points possible.
3 of a Kind Three of the same number. The points scored are the total for that turn (all five dice). Up to 30 points possible.
Four of a Kind Four of the same number. The points scored are the total for that turn (all five dice). Up to 30 points possible.
Full House Three of a kind and a pair. 25 points.
Small Straight Four sequential dice. 30 points.
Large Straight Five sequential dice. 40 points.
YachtZoe! Five of a kind. 50 points.
Chance Can be anything. Score is the sum of all dice in that turn. Up to 30 points possible.
